### Composition of Gastric Juice (30-06-2018)

1.  **Hydrochloric acid (HCl)**
    *   It neutralizes the function of salivary amylase that comes from the mouth.
    *   It denatures the ptyalin enzyme.
    *   It provides an acidic medium for the best functioning of protease enzyme.
    *   It kills pathogens (bacteria) from swallowed (ingested) food.
    *   It also converts the inactive pepsinogen into active enzyme called pepsin.
    *   The pH inside the stomach ranges between $2.0$ to $3.0$.

2.  **Enzyme**
    *   **Pepsin enzyme:** An enzyme which catalyzes the hydrolysis of protein into peptone or polypeptide.
        Protein + H$_2$O $\xrightarrow{\text{Pepsin}}$ Peptone (polypeptide)
    *   The difference between protein and peptone is the length of the amino acid chain.
    *   **Renin enzyme:** In young mammals, renin is needed to coagulate the soluble protein in milk.
        Coagulated (solidified) soluble milk protein is called casein.

3.  **Mucus**
    *   Protects the lining of the stomach wall from being digested by protease enzyme.

**Chyme:** The soup-like, uniform to creamy paste that is the food that leaves the stomach.
The movement of chyme is regulated by the pyloric sphincter muscle.
