[[BIO-U5 - Digestion in the Small Intestine]]
Where does the majority of chemical digestion and absorption take place?
A) The stomach
B) The large intestine
C) The small intestine
D) The esophagus
Correct Answer: C
---
Which part is considered the upper part of the small intestine?
A) Jejunum
B) Ileum
C) Rectum
D) Duodenum
Correct Answer: D
---
What is the primary function of the duodenum?
A) It connects the small intestine to the large intestine.
B) It is where the majority of chemical digestion takes place.
C) It stores bile temporarily.
D) It absorbs fatty acids and glycerol.
Correct Answer: B
---

A) Jejunum
B) Cecum
C) Appendix
D) Pancreas
Correct Answer: A

A) Duodenum
B) Jejunum
