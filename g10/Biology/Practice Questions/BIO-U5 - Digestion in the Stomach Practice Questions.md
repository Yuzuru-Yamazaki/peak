[[BIO-U5 - Digestion in the Stomach]]
What are the two primary functions of the stomach mentioned in the text?
A) Digestion of starch and absorption of water
B) Food storage and initial protein digestion
C) Production of bile and elimination of waste
D) Chewing of food and regulation of saliva
Correct Answer: B
