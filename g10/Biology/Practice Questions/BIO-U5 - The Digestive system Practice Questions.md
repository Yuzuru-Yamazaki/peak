[[BIO-U5 - The Digestive system]]
What is the primary goal of the digestive process on food particles?
A) To make them larger and more complex.
B) To change them from a soluble to an insoluble form.
C) To change them from a large, insoluble form to a smaller, soluble form.
D) To combine them with metabolic wastes for removal.
Correct Answer: C
---
Which of the following processes is defined as taking nutrients into the mouth?
A) Digestion
B) Ingestion
C) Assimilation
D) Absorption
Correct Answer: B
---
The transport of digested nutrients from the small intestine into the body's cells is called:
A) Egestion
B) Ingestion
C) Assimilation
D) Absorption
Correct Answer: D
---
What is the ultimate use of the end products of digestion, such as providing energy or building cell parts?
A) Absorption
B) Assimilation
C) Egestion
D) Excretion
Correct Answer: B
---
The removal of undigested food waste from the body is known as:
A) Excretion
B) Digestion
C) Egestion
D) Assimilation
Correct Answer: C
---
How is excretion different from egestion?
A) Excretion removes undigested food, while egestion removes metabolic wastes.
B) Excretion is the use of nutrients by cells, while egestion is their removal.
C) Excretion removes metabolic wastes, while egestion removes undigested food.
D) There is no difference between the two processes.
Correct Answer: C
---
Which of the following correctly lists the path of food through the alimentary canal?
A) Mouth → Esophagus → Stomach → Large Intestine → Small Intestine
B) Mouth → Stomach → Esophagus → Small Intestine → Large Intestine
C) Mouth → Esophagus → Stomach → Small Intestine → Large Intestine
D) Esophagus → Mouth → Stomach → Rectum → Anus
Correct Answer: C
---
Immediately after leaving the stomach, food enters which organ?
A) Esophagus
B) Large Intestine
C) Rectum
D) Small Intestine
Correct Answer: D
---
What are the finger-like projections called villi used for?
A) To move food from the esophagus to the stomach.
B) To remove metabolic wastes from cells.
C) To transport digested nutrients from the small intestine to the body's cells.
D) To store undigested food waste in the rectum.

A) Excretion

Which structure in the digestive tract immediately precedes the large intestine?
A) Stomach
